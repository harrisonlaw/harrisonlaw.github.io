Caroline Harrison - Solicitor/Director
Caroline is our founder and director and has oversight of all Legal work at Harrison Legal.
A specialist in Family and Child Law, Caroline regularly represents our clients at both Children's Panels and in sheriff courts across Scotland. Acting on behalf of children she fiercely defends the interests of her clients and is a highly skilled and competent litigator.
Caroline delivers a consistently excellent service to all of her clients and has an emphatic manner which is well suited to the emotive and sensitive areas of law that she practices within.

Lindsay Dempsey - Office Manager
Lindsay has extensive experience of the legal industry having previously worked in another local solicitors and an estate agency in the wider Ayrshire area. Lindsay is well versed in document preperation, legal process, office management and scheduling. Being a family woman herself, Lindsay knows that your children are the heart of your world and how important it is that we assist you quickly and efficiently.

Holly Hepburn - Trainee Solicitor
Holly is the newest addition to our team here at Harrison Legal, joining us as a Trainee Solicitor mid 2020.
Holly is ready and prepared to help our clients in all manner of matters from wills to contract disputes.
Since joining us Holly has already formed strong relationships with a number of our clients and looks forward to fighting your corner along with the rest of our legal team.

Luke Light - Legal Assistant
Luke joined our team at the beginning of 2020. Luke currently works as our Legal Assistant and is often the direct contact to clients for legal aid, statements and paperwork preparation. Luke is well known to our clients for his vigour and passion about helping in their cases and assisting Ms Harrison.
Luke obtained his dual LLB Honours degree in Scots and English Law in 2020 and is currently completing his diploma in legal practice - his last step before beginning his traineeship to become a fully qualified solicitor.
Passionate about all areas of Family Law and Private Client, Luke is a fierce advocate for mental health law
and representation.
