Caroline qualified as a solicitor in 2009. She worked as an Associate in two of the largest legal aid firms in Scotland throughout her eight years working in Glasgow. She moved down to Ayrshire and started working in a firm in
Kilmarnock in 2017. Caroline's main area of expertise is Family and Child Law.

Caroline regularly attends at Children’s Hearings and local Sheriff Courts representing the interests of her clients,
demonstrating her vast experience in raising and defending court proceedings.
Acting on behalf of children she fiercely defends the interests of her clients and is a highly skilled and competent litigator. Caroline delivers a consistently excellent service to all of her clients and has an empathetic manner which is well suited to the emotive and sensitive areas of law that she practices within.

Also working in the firm is Lindsay Dempsey, Office Manager. Lindsay has been Caroline’s right hand woman since her move to Kilmarnock and has excellent people skills. Lindsay knows all clients cases and is able to speak to clients and answers the majority of queries whilst Caroline is at court.

Chris is our legal assistant who is in regular contact with the clients updating them in respect of their cases and taking statements from witnesses. If you wish to contact him then please do so on
chris@harrisonlaw.legal or 07984 331518.

We carry out legal aid work and private work. We can carry out a financial calculation over the telephone with you to establish your eligibility prior to your appointment.
