Social Work Involvement

1


Overview

Social Workers can be involved with families on a voluntary or statutory basis. In both circumstances we can provide you with appropriate advice to ensure that you and your family are receiving the support your need whilst being treated fairly.

We can link in with Advocacy Services to ensure that you do not have to attend meetings alone as these meetings can often be very challenging.
